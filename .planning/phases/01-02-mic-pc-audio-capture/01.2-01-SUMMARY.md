## Implementation Details

- **AudioCapture**: Captures audio from PC microphone using sounddevice
- **VoiceActivityDetector**: Energy-based VAD with configurable sensitivity
- **AudioBuffer**: Stores audio chunks (max 10 seconds)
- **MicController**: Orchestrates all components with `listen()` blocking method

## Key Features

- Non-blocking audio capture in background thread
- VAD with callbacks for voice start/end events
- Configurable sample rate, chunk size, and thresholds
- Easy integration with STT modules
